#### Part I: Probability Theory
*   **Fundamental Laws:** Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson, Geometric, Hypergeometric.
*   **Continuous Distributions:** Uniform, Normal (Gaussian), Exponential, Gamma, Chi-Square ($x^2$), Student's t, Fisher (F).
*   **Theorems:**
    *   **Law of Total Probability & Bayes' Theorem.**
    *   **CLT (Central Limit Theorem):** Why averages of samples distribute normally.
    *   **Inequalities:** Markov and Chebyshev.
*   **Markov Chains:** Transition probabilities, predicting future states based on current state.

#### Part II: Statistics
*   **Descriptive:** Mean, Median, Mode, Variance, Standard Deviation, Quartiles.
*   **Inferential:**
    *   **Estimation:** Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE), Method of Moments.
    *   **Confidence Intervals:** Estimating population parameters within a range.
*   **Hypothesis Testing:**
    *   $H_0$ (Null) vs $H_1$ (Alternative).
    *   **P-value:** The probability of obtaining results at least as extreme as observed results.
    *   **Tests:** Z-test (known variance), T-test (unknown variance), F-test (compare variances).
